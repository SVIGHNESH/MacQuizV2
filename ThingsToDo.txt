The email leg: quiz.EmailSender (server/internal/quiz/email.go) is a small Send(ctx, to, toName, subject, textBody) interface, decoupled from any provider the same way EventPublisher/SnapshotCache are (quiz never imports net/http); quiz.Service.emailAssignmentChanges (lifecycle.go), called from SetAssignments right after the existing PublishNotify loop, sends one email per student whose audience membership actually changed - an "Assigned: {title}" mail for a newly-added student (reusing assignedStudents' post-commit query for name/email, no extra round trip), a "Removed: {title}" mail for a dropped one (a small usersByID lookup, since a dropped student is no longer in that query's result).
